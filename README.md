The primary goal of this analysis was to help an investor create a new football team by defining the most suitable player types and playing style to maximize success, using historical match data, team statistics, and individual player stats. The project included rigorous data quality reporting and the development of descriptive visualizations—such as defensive heat maps and goal distribution graphs—which helped identify key patterns and communicate findings effectively. The final deliverables included a comprehensive executive report, an investor presentation, and a Databricks notebook. The work provided two main benefits: a deep understanding of the league and its players, and a data-driven strategic guide for building a competitive team in the English league.
